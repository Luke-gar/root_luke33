Luke's Capython

Discription

one may be asking what a capython is? my teachers at Vanier college decided to run a sort of hackathon/project week. within this week firt years, second years and third years would all work togehter in order to create a project to the best of their ability.

so for this capython we decided to create a google event page, where people can futher learn about indigenous communities we went about this by createing a 3d model of a isle with grayscale models of items associated with cultural significance. once the user would press on the greyscale models it would play a video that we animated, edited and modeled within the week explaining the significance of these items in their culture.

My part

so during this project, I created some of the models for the isle, created a single character, and basically made and set up the isle that you see in the home page, i also created some background elements but it was uneeded in the end so we scraped them.
