How to draw, a Luke walk through.

Discription

This is a website for my project at vanier college. The goal for this website was to be able to demonstrate my capabilites and understanding with html, css, javascript and general conceptual design. I went about this by utalizing animations shown to me in class along side javascript. The content that I chose to fill the website was a walkthrough on how to draw.

3 websites

As a way of gathering my thoughts on what I was looking for in desgin for the website I found these 3 websites: icelandicexplorer neal deepsea rewildyourself. It may not be exact, but I fell in love with the infinite scrolling, and a lot of the fading in animations.

Inspieration* and mood board

As for most of my Inspieration, I believe when we have alot of freedom like in this project our hobbies tend to come out and show themselves. in this case i'm aware of how much of a superfan I am of anime and art so i drew Inspieration from a manga i have read named vagabond and a trend online called hopecore. as a way of hopefully incouraging younger artists to continue on their path.
